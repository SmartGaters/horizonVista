The Business Problem

Why it matters

Travel inquiries arrive when human agents are offline, busy, or working in a different language.

Pain points

  • Slow replies turn high-intent customers into missed bookings.
  • Telegram, website chat, Instagram, and WhatsApp fragment the sales process.
  • Pricing changes often because of seasons, routes, and currency.
  • Manual complaint handling gives weak visibility to management.

Business risk

  • Revenue leaks after hours.
  • Customer experience varies by agent availability.
  • Leads are not always captured with complete details.
  • Operational quality is hard to measure consistently.
